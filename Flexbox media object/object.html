Paper Bird Tutorial

Learn how to create a beautiful paper bird with these simple steps.

  1. Get a square piece of paper.
  2. Fold the paper in half diagonally and then unfold it.
  3. Fold both sides of the paper to the center line.
  4. Fold the top triangle of paper behind the model.
  5. Fold both the top corners down and to the center along the dotted lines.
  6. Open up the paper trapped inside and pull it out and down.
  7. Fold a large X into the paper.
  8. Turn the paper over.
  9. Fold a large + into the paper.
  10. Bring all of the corners in toward the center.
  11. Flatten the paper into a square.
  12. Fold the top edges of the diamond inwards toward the center line.

Paper Butterfly Tutorial

Create a delicate paper butterfly with these easy instructions.

  1. Start with a square piece of paper, colored side up.
  2. Fold the paper in half diagonally and then unfold it.
  3. Fold the paper in half diagonally in the opposite direction and then unfold it.
  4. Fold the paper in half horizontally and vertically and then unfold it.
  5. Bring all four corners into the center to form a smaller square.
  6. Fold the top layer down to the bottom point.
  7. Turn the paper over and fold the top point down to the bottom point.
  8. Fold the sides in to form the butterfly's wings.
  9. Adjust the folds to shape the butterfly as desired.

Paper Goggles Tutorial

Craft your own paper goggles with these simple steps.

  1. Get a rectangular piece of paper.
  2. Fold the paper in half lengthwise and then unfold it.
  3. Fold both sides of the paper to the center line.
  4. Fold the top corners down to the center line, forming two triangles.
  5. Fold the bottom edge up to meet the center line.
  6. Fold the paper in half along the center line, with the triangles on the outside.
  7. Cut out two circles in the middle of the triangles for the lenses.
  8. Fold the outer edges of the triangles inward to create the sides of the goggles.
  9. Adjust the folds for a comfortable fit.
  10. Decorate the goggles as desired.
